Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 3, 2025

1. Introduction

ProphecyML is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

We collect your name and email address via Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication purposes. We do not collect passwords or other sensitive data.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to identify you within the app, enforce rate limits, and personalize your experience. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

Authentication tokens are securely stored and encrypted. We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide services.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google OAuth and Alpaca API. Please refer to their privacy policies for more information.

6. User Rights

You may revoke access via your Google account settings. Contact us to request deletion or access to your data.
